Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27 November 2025

Welcome to Punjabi Vyakaran, an educational app published by Royal Pixel Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Introduction

Punjabi Vyakaran is an educational application designed to help users learn the rules of Punjabi grammar. We respect your privacy and ensure that the app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information.

Specifically:

  • No name, email, phone number, or profile information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No analytics, crash reports, or usage tracking

3. How the App Works

The app displays Punjabi grammar topics and explanations. Some content is fetched from the web to keep topics updated. This process does not identify or track the user in any way.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for hosting content. These services may have their own privacy policies, which you can review independently. The app itself does not send any personal information to these services.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is safe for all age groups as it collects no data whatsoever.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
